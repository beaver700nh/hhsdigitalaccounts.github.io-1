Plants acquire nutrients from the soil and air.


Discover the role of nutrients in plants.

OBJECTIVE: to explore the role of carbon dioxide and the major mineral nutrients in plant growth

Plants use the energy from light and the carbon in carbon dioxide to manufacture their own food. For proper growth, plants, like animals, also require important elements found in mineral nutrients.
