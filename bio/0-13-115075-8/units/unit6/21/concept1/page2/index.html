Plants acquire nutrients from the soil and air.


Discover the role of nutrients in plants.

OBJECTIVE: to explore the source of nitrogen for plant growth

Plants need nitrogen for the production of proteins and nucleic acids. Although nitrogen gas (N2) makes up 78 percent of the atmosphere, plants can't use this unreactive form of nitrogen; the nitrogen must be converted into a chemically useable compound.
