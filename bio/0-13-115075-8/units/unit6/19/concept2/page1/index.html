Mosses and other bryophytes were the first land plants.


Explore mosses.

OBJECTIVE: to examine the diversity, characteristics, and habitat of bryophytes

Mosses and their relatives are small plants restricted to moist habitats. By comparing the adaptations of mosses to other groups of land plants, scientists hypothesize that the mosses were among the first plants to colonize land environments.
