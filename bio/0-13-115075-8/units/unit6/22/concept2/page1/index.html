Plants respond to changes in the environment.


Activate phototropism.

OBJECTIVE: to investigate the effects of different wavelengths of light on plant growth

Plants usually grow toward a source of light (phototropism). Remember that visible light is made up of wavelengths of energy that you see as colors. Do plants demonstrate phototropism to all the wavelengths of light (colors), or do plants respond to one or several particular colors? In this activity, you will investigate these questions.
