Plants respond to changes in the environment.


Activate phototropism.

OBJECTIVE: to investigate the role of light and gravity on the direction of stem growth

Shoots grow toward the light in a response called phototropism. They also grow away from the pull of gravity in a response called gravitropism. Under normal conditions, the gravity and the light work together and the plant grows upward, out of the soil. What happens to stem growth if light and gravity act in opposite directions?
