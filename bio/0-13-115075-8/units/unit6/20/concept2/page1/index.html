Structure fits function in the plant body.


Identify plant tissues.

OBJECTIVE: to explore the variation in structure and function of different plant organs

Plants have only three organs: roots, stems, and leaves. In addition, flowering plants contain structures that function in sexual reproduction. The appearance and function of these organs can differ greatly among species. After reviewing the basic plant structures, you will determine which organ various vegetables represent.
