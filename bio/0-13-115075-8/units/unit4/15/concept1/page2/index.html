OBJECTIVE: to explore the tempo of speciation by investigating the fossil history of elephants

The evolutionary history of elephants is relatively well understood because their fossilized skeletons are large and easy to find. The fossil record indicates that elephants have undergone a recent and rapid evolution that demonstrates the punctuated equilibrium model of evolution.
