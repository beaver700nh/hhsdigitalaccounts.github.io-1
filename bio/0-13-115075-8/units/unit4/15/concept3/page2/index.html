Solve a fossil mystery.

OBJECTIVE: to explore the history of life on Earth

By studying the fossil record, scientists have put together a geologic time scale divided into four ages known as the Precambrian, Paleozoic, Mesozoic, and Cenozoic eras. Each era represents a distinct step in the evolutionary history of life. Explore the eras in this activity. When you have finished the activity, scroll down and answer the questions below.

Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete each answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

1. According to the fossil record, animals evolved during which era?

2. The activity shows a representation of continental drift—Earth's landmasses moving and separating over time. How do you think this movement influenced the evolution of living things?

3. What defines the eras? Explain.
