Locate homologous structures.

OBJECTIVE: to examine the similarities in homologous structures

The forelimbs of mammals are an example of homologous structures. When scientists compare the forelimb bones in different animals, they look for similarities in the detailed structures of the bones. Even if the forelimb bones have different functions, they support a common origin among the animals because it is so unlikely that they would look so similar otherwise, especially given the different functions.
