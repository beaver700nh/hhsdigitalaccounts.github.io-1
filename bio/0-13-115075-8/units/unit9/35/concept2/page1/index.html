There are limits to population growth.


Analyze population growth data.

OBJECTIVE: to calculate exponential growth of bacteria

In this activity, you will observe exponential growth in action. During exponential growth, a population multiplies by a constant factor at constant time intervals. A population that is experiencing exponential growth is not limited by any factors. In this bacteria example, the constant factor for the population is two because each parent cell splits and forms two offspring cells. Scroll down and complete the following activity.
