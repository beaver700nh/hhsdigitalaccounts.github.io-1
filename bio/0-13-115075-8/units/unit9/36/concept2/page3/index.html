OBJECTIVE: to build an energy pyramid that illustrates the loss of the available energy between trophic levels

On the average, only about 10 percent of the energy (kcal) in organic matter in one trophic level is passed to the next higher level. This relationship is often depicted as a pyramid, which you will create in this exercise.
