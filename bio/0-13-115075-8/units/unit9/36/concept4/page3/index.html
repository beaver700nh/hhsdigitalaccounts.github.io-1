Human activities can alter ecosystems.


Analyze effects of deforestation.

OBJECTIVE: to explore how nitrogen cycling is strongly influenced by vegetation in a forest ecosystem

In this activity, you will add new data to your graphs. This data is taken from a watershed, called Watershed 6, which is similar to Watershed 5. The data from Watershed 6 is called reference data because Watershed 6, unlike Watershed 5, has not been clear-cut. You will compare the nitrate concentration in stream and soil water from both watersheds to determine if the nitrate concentration is affected by the loss of vegetation due to clear-cutting.
