Human activities can alter ecosystems.


Analyze effects of deforestation.

OBJECTIVE: to plot and analyze actual data for the nitrate concentration of soil and stream in the Hubbard Brook Forest

Since 1962 a team of scientists at Hubbard Brook Forest have been studying nutrient cycling in the forested ecosystem. In the following activity, you will plot and analyze actual nitrate data collected from soil and stream water of Watershed 5 during the month of June from 1982 to 1990.

Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete each answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

1. What happens to the nitrate concentration in both stream and soil in the years 1984 to 1986 compared to the level before the clear-cut in 1983?

2. In what year did nitrate concentration return to pre-cut levels?

3. What is the relationship of the trees and plants to the levels of nitrates in stream and soil water?

4. What do you think could be a negative consequence of the increased levels of nitrate concentration in stream water?
