OBJECTIVE: to examine the diversity, characteristics, and habitat of crustraceans

Crustaceans are common, mostly aquatic arthropods that include the decapods, barnacles, copepods, and isopods. You are probably most familiar with the decapods—lobster, crab, and shrimp—but you may also recognize a land-dwelling member of the isopod group called the pill bug.
