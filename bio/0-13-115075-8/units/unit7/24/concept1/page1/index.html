Build an arthropod.

OBJECTIVE: to examine the basic structures of an arthropod

Arthropods are very important members of Earth's ecosystems. In most ecosystems, the most common and diverse animals are arthropods. In this activity, you'll explore some of the basic structures found in many arthropods by studying the external body parts of an insect. In addition to these structures, remember that the entire arthropod body is covered by a hard external skeleton, or exoskeleton. When you finish, scroll down and answer the questions below.
