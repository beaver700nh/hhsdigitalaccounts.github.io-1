OBJECTIVE: to explore how the resting potential of a neuron's membrane is maintained

In this concept, you learn about the potential energy of a resting neuron. Recall that because opposite charges are attracted to each other, separating them is a form of potential energy. When a neuron is not transmitting a signal, its plasma membrane keeps opposite charges apart, creating a "resting potential" until a nerve signal is transmitted. In this activity, you will measure the voltage across the membrane of a neuron at rest and explore the mechanisms that maintain this voltage.
