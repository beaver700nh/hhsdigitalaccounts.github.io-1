Action Potentials

OBJECTIVE: to analyze the electrical changes associated with an action potential.

Turning on a flashlight uses stored energy to produce light. Similarly, stimulating a neuron can release the membrane's potential energy to generate an action potential. In this activity, you will analyze the changes in the membrane voltage that occur as a neuron is stimulated. When you finish the activity, scroll down and answer the questions below.
