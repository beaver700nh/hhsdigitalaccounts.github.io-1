OBJECTIVE: to identify the major endocrine glands and their functions

Hormones are molecules that are released into the bloodstream and carried to other parts of the body, where they trigger specific responses. They are produced by endocrine glands and play an important role in homeostasis. In this activity, you will identify the major endocrine glands and learn about their functions.
