OBJECTIVE: to explore the four processes involved in urine formation

The formation of urine in the kidney is a complex process involving the filtration of blood. Blood enters the kidneys and eventually moves through a capillary system to the microscopic nephrons. The four stages of blood processing and urine formation are filtration, reabsorption, secretion, and excretion. In this activity, you will explore each of these processes.
