Introduction
To start your investigation, you will find out more about the functions of reproductive tubules in male and female humans and other mammals. In the male reproductive system, sperm cells are formed, stored, and transported within tubules. Fertilization occurs when a sperm penetrates an egg (oocyte) in a tubule within the female reproductive system. Each tubule has structural features that relate to its function in reproduction. In the lab, you will use a microscope to observe these structural features and identify the reproductive tubules.
