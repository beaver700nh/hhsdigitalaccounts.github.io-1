OBJECTIVE: to investigate causes and effects of specific bacterial and viral STDs

Diseases that are spread by sexual contact are called sexually transmitted diseases, or STDs. In this activity, you will investigate the causes, symptoms, statistics, treatments, and consequences of not treating various STDs.
