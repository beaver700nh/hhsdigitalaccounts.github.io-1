Embryonic and fetal development occurs in the uterus.


Trace an embryo's development.

OBJECTIVE: to follow the early development of the human embryo

Seven days after fertilization, the embryo, now in the blastocyst stage, implants into the uterine wall. It is composed of an "inner cell mass" and a surrounding layer of cells called the trophoblast. In this activity, you will assist the physician as she performs several ultrasounds on women at various stages during their pregnancies.
