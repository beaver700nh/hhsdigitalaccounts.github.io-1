Embryonic and fetal development occurs in the uterus.


Trace an embryo's development.

OBJECTIVE: to follow and examine the developmental progression of an embryo and fetus

Health-care providers use ultrasound technology to produce moving images of a developing fetus. By analyzing these images, physicians can assess the fetus for size, age, gender, and possible abnormalities. In this activity, you will assist the technician as she performs several ultrasounds on women at various stages during their pregnancies.
