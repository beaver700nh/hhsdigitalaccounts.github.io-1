OBJECTIVE: to build the skeleton of the human body

In the human body, the axial skeleton consists of the bones that form the upright portion, or axis, of the body. This includes the skull, vertebral column, sternum, and ribs. The appendicular skeleton includes the pelvic girdle (or hip), shoulder girdle (the scapula and clavicle), and bones of the arms and legs.
