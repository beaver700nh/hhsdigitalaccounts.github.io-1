OBJECTIVE: to analyze a heartbeat and the electrical activity of the heart

A "heartbeat" is one complete cycle of contraction and relaxation of the heart chambers. Each heartbeat lasts approximately 0.8 seconds and repeats itself about 70 times per minute. In this activity, you will explore the phases of a heartbeat and the heart's electrical activity, which controls the cycle. Note that although an actual heartbeat lasts 0.8 seconds, the real time of this heartbeat animation is longer so that the events can be clearly shown.
