OBJECTIVE: to explore the cardiovascular disease atherosclerosis and how it can lead to a heart attack

Like all your body cells, heart muscle cells require oxygen and nutrient-rich blood to survive. When blood exits the heart through the aorta, several coronary arteries immediately branch off, feeding the cardiac muscle tissue. If one or more of the heart's coronary arteries become blocked, heart muscles cells will quickly die. Such an event is called a heart attack.
