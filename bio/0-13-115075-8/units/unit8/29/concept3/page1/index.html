Calculate Calories and interpret a food label.

OBJECTIVE: to understand the relationship between Calorie intake and energy needs

Food provides the basic fuel and nutrients required for maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle. Most female teenagers need approximately 2,200 Calories per day. Most male teenagers need about 2,500 Calories per day. Consuming more or less Calories may affect a person's weight over time. In this activity, you will estimate daily Caloric intake and then explore the relationships among Caloric intake, activity level, and weight gain or loss.
