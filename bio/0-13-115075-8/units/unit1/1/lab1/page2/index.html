Introduction
To start your investigation you will learn the basic steps that you need to follow in order to make observations with a microscope. In this lab you will observe one type of organism from each of the four kingdoms in the domain Eukarya: plants, animals, protists, and fungi. Although these organisms are all very different, one characteristic that they share is that they all consist of one or more eukaryotic cells. A eukaryotic cell contains a membrane-enclosed nucleus that separates genetic material from the rest of the cell, while prokaryotic cells do not contain a membrane-enclosed nucleus.
