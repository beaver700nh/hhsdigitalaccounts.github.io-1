Investigate how birds learn songs.

OBJECTIVE: to investigate how white-crowned sparrows learn songs

Male songbirds, like the white-crowned sparrow, announce their breeding territories with song. Most birds' songs are specific to that species. Do songbirds learn their songs or are they somehow programmed at birth? Researchers wanted to know this, so they raised young sparrows in a laboratory, isolating them from wild sparrows. The laboratory sparrows could not hear the songs of wild adult sparrows, and therefore had no models. Could they learn the songs? Find out in this activity.
