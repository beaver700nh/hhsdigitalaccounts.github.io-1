Hypothesis-based science is a search for explanations.


Form a hypothesis.

OBJECTIVE: to use scientific observations to formulate a hypothesis and design an experiment to test this hypothesis

Observations and inferences that scientists make help them ask questions and eventually formulate hypotheses. Experiments to test hypotheses are then designed by scientists to help explain what they observed. Use the observations and inferences you made on the previous page to create your own hypothesis, then run an experiment to test the hypothesis.
