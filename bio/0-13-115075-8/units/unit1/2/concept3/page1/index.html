Trace a famous scientific discovery.

OBJECTIVE: to trace the discovery of a famous scientific model

Scientific research is motivated by questions. Often the answer to a question leads to new questions, which in turn lead to insights beyond the scope of the original research. In this activity, you'll explore one such story. It begins with the search for the "controlling factor," a substance that scientists hypothesized controls all of a cell's activities. Once this molecule was identified, scientists realized that it was critical to understand the molecule's precise structure. That knowledge in turn sparked a quest to map the human genome, the complete set of genetic material in the nucleus of every human cell.
