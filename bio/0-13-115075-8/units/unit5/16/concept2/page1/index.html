Diverse prokaryotes populate the biosphere.


Determine modes of nutrition.

OBJECTIVE: to investigate the ecological niches of various prokaryotes (bacteria and archaea) and determine their modes of nutrition

The means by which prokaryotic organisms obtain energy and carbon (modes of nutrition) can be quite different. In this activity, you will review the four modes of nutrition into which prokaryotes and other organisms can be classified, and then learn about various prokaryotic organisms to determine their particular "lifestyles."
