OBJECTIVE: to calculate fungal growth rate

The term fungi may make you think of mushrooms. However, mushrooms, which are the reproductive structures of certain fungi, are only a small part of the overall fungus body. The bodies of most fungi are large mats of threads known as hyphae, which absorb nutrients. In this activity, you will explore the growth of hyphae.
