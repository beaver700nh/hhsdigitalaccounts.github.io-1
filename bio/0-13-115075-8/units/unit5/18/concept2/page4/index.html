OBJECTIVE: to examine the diversity, characteristics, and habitat of club fungi

Most people think first of mushrooms and toadstools when they think of fungi. The club fungi produce mushrooms as reproductive structures. Some club fungi are extremely poisonous, while others are valued additions to many menus. Find out more about club fungi in this activity.
