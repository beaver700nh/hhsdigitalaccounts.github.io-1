Biologists can genetically engineer plants and animals.


Model a cloning procedure.

OBJECTIVE: to model and analyze a cloning procedure

Recently the first-ever cloned mouse, named Cumulina, received her DNA from a donor mouse, which means that she was genetically identical to the donor. In this activity, you will follow the basic procedure scientists use to clone mammals by virtually cloning a mouse. When you have finished, scroll down and answer the questions below.

Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete each answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

1. Describe the roles of the three mice in the cloning procedure.

2. Explain why the scientists chose to clone a mouse with brown fur, but took the egg from a mouse with white fur.

3. Why is it unnecessary to fertilize the egg to produce an embryo?
