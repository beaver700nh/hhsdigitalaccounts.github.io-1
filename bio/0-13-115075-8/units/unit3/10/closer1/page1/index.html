Closer Look: Linked Genes

OBJECTIVE: to understand how the probability of a crossover occurring between two genes is affected by the distance between the two genes

In this activity, you will look at three linked genes and examine the probability of new recombinations. When you finish, scroll down and answer the questions below.

Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete the answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

1. Are there more crossovers between gene C and gene D or between gene D and gene E?

2. Why do you expect more crossovers between genes D and E?

3. Assume the distance between genes C and D is 10 units and the distance between genes D and E is 30 units. If you scored 5 crossovers between genes C and D, how many crossovers would you expect to occur between genes D and E?
