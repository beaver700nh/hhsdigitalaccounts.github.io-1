OBJECTIVE: to investigate DNA replication

DNA is composed of two complementary strands (A pairs with T, and G pairs with C). Therefore, the sequence of bases of one strand can be used to make a new complementary strand. In the activity below, notice that when each strand is copied the result is two identical DNA molecules. When you finish the activity, scroll down to answer the questions below.
