Interpret a pedigree.

OBJECTIVE: to create and analyze a pedigree in order to determine the pattern of inheritance for a particular trait

By tracing the expression of a certain trait through several generations, geneticists can usually determine the pattern of inheritance of that trait. In this activity, you will build a pedigree by examining the data collected from several generations, and then try to determine whether the trait is dominant or recessive.
