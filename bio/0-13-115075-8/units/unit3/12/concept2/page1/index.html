Accidents affecting chromosomes can cause disorders.


Discover effects of chromosome changes.

OBJECTIVE: to investigate karyotypes and identify changes in chromosome numbers

In meiosis, gametes end up with a haploid set of chromosomes. Occasionally something goes wrong, and a gamete can receive an abnormal set of chromosomes. In this activity, you will examine several human karyotypes and investigate the mechanism that causes certain mistakes. Before you begin, you may find it helpful to first review Online Activity 9.5: Explore the process of meiosis in depth.
