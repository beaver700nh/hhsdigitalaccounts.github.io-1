OBJECTIVE: to investigate chromosome numbers in the human egg and sperm cell

Humans normally have 46 chromosomes (23 pairs) in their body cells, as the karyotype shows. But how did the body cells receive this particular number? In this activity, you will investigate chromosome numbers in the human egg and sperm cell. Recall that the egg and sperm unite to form a zygote. Analyze the activity below, and then scroll down to answer the questions.
