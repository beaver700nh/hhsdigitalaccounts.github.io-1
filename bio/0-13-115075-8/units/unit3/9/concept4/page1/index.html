Cancer cells grow and divide out of control.


Examine the cycle of a cancer cell.

OBJECTIVE: to observe what happens when the control system in the cell cycle malfunctions

The cell cycle is an orderly sequence of events that is directed by certain proteins within the cell. This control system regulates the cell processes at several "checkpoints." In this activity, you will see what happens when the control system malfunctions, and cells grow and divide uncontrollably, causing a condition called cancer.
