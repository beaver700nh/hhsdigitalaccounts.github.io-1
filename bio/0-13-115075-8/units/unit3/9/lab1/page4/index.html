In the lab procedure, you will prepare microscope slides of cells from onion root tips. The photos below show some of the steps you will perform in the lab. If you'd like a closer look at any ofthe photos, click on the image. A new browser window will open. Be sure to close the new browser window when you are finished.

You will use a single edge razor blade to cut four root tips off an onion bulb, as shown. Each root tip should be about two centimeters (2 cm) long.




You will place the root tips in a test tube and cover them a small amount of fixative solution. This solution is highly acidic, so make sure to follow all safety guidelines.




To help the fixative solution penetrate the cells, you will place your test tube in a warm water bath at 50°C for several minutes.
