Cells divide during the mitotic phase.


Observe mitosis in action.

OBJECTIVE: to examine how cells divide in a process called mitosis

In Online Activity 9.2, you learned about the phases of the cell cycle. Some cells do not complete the cell cycle. Instead, they enter the M phase and undergo nuclear division (mitosis) and cell division (cytokinesis). In this activity, you will examine the preparation and the process involved in the actual nuclear and cytoplasmic division of a cell.
