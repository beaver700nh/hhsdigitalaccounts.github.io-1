Explore light energy in action.

OBJECTIVE: to follow the steps involved in photosynthesis and respiration

All organisms need energy to grow, develop, and maintain a living state. This energy is acquired from the chemical energy in food through a process called cellular respiration. A typical plant makes its own food through photosynthesis, while an animal obtains food by eating producers and/or other consumers.
