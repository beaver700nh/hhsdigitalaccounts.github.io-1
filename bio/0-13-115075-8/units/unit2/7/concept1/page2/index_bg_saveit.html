Sunlight powers life.




All organisms need energy for metabolism — to grow, develop, and maintain a living state. This energy is acquired from the chemical energy in food through a process called cellular respiration.

A typical animal (consumer) obtains food by eating, while a typical plant (producer) makes its own food by photosynthesis.
