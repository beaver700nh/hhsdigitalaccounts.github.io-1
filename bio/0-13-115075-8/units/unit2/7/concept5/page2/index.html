Cellular respiration converts energy in food to energy in ATP.


Explore a pinball analogy for cellular respiration.

OBJECTIVE: to compare the output of the three stages of respiration

How to Play: Scroll down to reveal the entire pinball animation. Put two ATP "coins" in the slot to start the game. Carefully follow the directions within the game. When you finish, answer the questions below.


Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete each answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

1. What is the final net gain of ATP molecules in the first stage, glycolysis?

2. How many ATP molecules were made in the second stage, the Krebs cycle?

3. How many ATP molecules were made in the third stage, the electron transport chain?
