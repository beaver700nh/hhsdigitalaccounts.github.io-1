Enzymes are proteins that speed up specific reactions in cells.


Investigate the role of enzymes in nature.

OBJECTIVE: to explore how a change in temperature affect an enzyme

Every enzyme has a specific shape with active sites that fit specific substrates. Does a change in temperature change an enzyme's effectiveness? Find out in this activity. When you finish, scroll down to answer the questions below.
