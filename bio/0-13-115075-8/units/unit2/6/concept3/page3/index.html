Membranes regulate the traffic of molecules.


Investigate movement across the membrane.

OBJECTIVE: to observe the processes of endocytosis and exocytosis

Large molecules and food particles cannot cross the plasma membrane. Instead they are packaged in vesicles, membrane sacs that move particles into (endocytosis) and out of (exocytosis) the cell.
