Investigate the structure and properties of water.

In this activity, you will explore a pond to discover how the polarity of water contributes to some fundamental processes in nature. When you finish the activity, scroll down to answer the questions below.

Answer the following questions in the boxes provided. After you complete each answer, click the Check Your Answer button for feedback.

2. How do the water molecules reach the top of the cattail?

3. Why is water a good solvent for ions, such as K+ and Na+?

4. Why does ice float on the surface of the pond in winter?
