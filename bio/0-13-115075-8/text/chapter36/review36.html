d. detritus.

a. omnivore.
b. carnivore.
c. herbivore.
d. quaternary consumer.

a. producer
b. primary consumer
c. secondary consumer
d. tertiary consumer

b. forest fires, transpiration, decomposers breaking down detritus

b. a decline in the phytoplankton population

c. eutrophication.
d. biomagnification.
