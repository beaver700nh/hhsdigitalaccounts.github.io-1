The essence of what makes a particular person genetically unique could be revealed in a simple pattern of glowing bands. The bands are produced by a technique that compares the genomes of different individuals by separating fragments of their DNA. Variations on this technique provide many useful types of information about genes and genomes.

More and more topics relating to genetic technologies are making news headlines. Many recent advances in agriculture, medicine, and forensics are based on techniques that isolate and manipulate DNA. This chapter will help you build a framework of concepts that you can use to interpret these news stories and be an informed citizen.
