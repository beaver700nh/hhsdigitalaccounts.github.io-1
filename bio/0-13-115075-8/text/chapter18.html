What do you picture when you think of a fungus? Though some fungi have bad reputations—like poisonous mushrooms or crop-destroying parasites—fungi serve an important role in ecosystems by decomposing the wastes and the remains of other living things.

As with other kingdoms you've studied in this unit, fungi are diverse in their appearance and habitat. Before you begin the chapter, go online to the WebQuest to go on a virtual mushroom hunt and learn about some interesting fungi.
