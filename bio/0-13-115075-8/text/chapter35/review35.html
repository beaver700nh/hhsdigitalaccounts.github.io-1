Choose the letter of the best answer.

24. Developing Hypotheses A scientist staked out two plots of land with the same five wildflower species in roughly equal numbers. Around one plot, she installed a fence to keep out kangaroo rats, which feed on wildflower seeds. After two years, four of the wildflower species were gone from the fenced-in plot, but one species had increased drastically. In the unfenced plot, all five types of flowers flourished. Suggest a hypothesis to explain these results.
