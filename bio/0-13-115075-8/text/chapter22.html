A spectacular display of wildflowers in the Anza-Borrego Desert of California happens about once every 10 years. Seeds of desert annuals are quite particular in their requirements for germination. Winter must have been neither too hot nor too cold, and spring rainfall must be relatively plentiful. Rainfall affects the ratio of plant hormones in the seed, as it washes out certain hormones that inhibit germination. From the very start of a plant's life, hormones play important roles. In this chapter, you will read about hormones and other factors affecting plant functions. Before you begin, go online to the WebQuest to learn about some unusual plant adaptations.
