Organizing Information
OBJECTIVE: to practice building and using concept maps   

  A concept map is a word diagram that shows how ideas, or concepts, are connected. The map typically consists of concepts (usually nouns, adjectives, or phrases) written inside ovals, squares, or circles, and connected with linking words and lines.
