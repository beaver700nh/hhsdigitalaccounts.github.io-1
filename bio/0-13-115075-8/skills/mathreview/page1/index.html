Math Review

OBJECTIVE: to review fractions, decimals, and percents and the relationships among these expressions

What does math have to do with biology? From comparing measurements of organisms to calculating growth rates to analyzing inheritance patterns, you will frequently rely on math skills during your study of living things. In this Skills Activity, you can review and practice some of the mathematical concepts that are especially useful in biology.

Fractions, decimals, and percents are all ways of expressing numerical information, and it is important to know how to convert from one expression to another. In this activity, you'll review each type of expression and practice moving among them.
