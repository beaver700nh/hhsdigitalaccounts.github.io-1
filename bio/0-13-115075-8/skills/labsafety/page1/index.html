Lab Safety Primer

OBJECTIVE: to review the meanings of lab safety symbols and determine if safety rules are being applied in example lab scenes

Safety in the biology laboratory is extremely important. As you prepare for a Biology: Exploring Life laboratory investigation, you may often see one or more safety symbols like those shown below. These symbols alert you to procedures that are hazardous. Understanding what the hazards are and how to prevent accidents will ensure your safety and the safety of others around you. In this activity, you will review the lab safety symbols and test your knowledge of these safety rules by evaluating several hypothetical laboratory scenarios.
